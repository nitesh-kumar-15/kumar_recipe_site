Cozy · 15 minutes

Upgraded Weeknight Ramen

Taking a humble pack of instant noodles and transforming it into a rich, complex, and deeply satisfying bowl of comfort using just a few fresh pantry staples.

Intro Ingredients Steps Tips

Act I · The Setup

Ditch the seasoning packet and build a quick broth with stock, soy sauce, and ginger. A soft‑boiled egg and handful of greens make it feel like a complete meal.

Act II · Ingredients

  • 1 pack instant ramen (discard the seasoning packet)
  • 2 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp grated ginger
  • 1 soft‑boiled egg
  • Handful of fresh spinach or bok choy

Act III · Steps

  1. In a small pot, bring the broth, soy sauce, and ginger to a rolling boil.
  2. Drop in the instant noodles and cook for 3 minutes.
  3. In the last minute of cooking, stir in the fresh greens until just wilted.
  4. Transfer to a bowl, drizzle with sesame oil, and slice the soft‑boiled egg in half to place on top.

Act IV · Tips & shortcuts

Keep a batch of marinated soft‑boiled eggs in the fridge specifically to drop into quick noodle bowls during the week. Add frozen corn or peas directly to the pot for extra texture.
